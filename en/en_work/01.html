An Email Template

Yani \ Jan 7th, Beijing

This is an universal email template prepared for myself to contact with professionals efficiently, and I would love to receive an email with clear expression and proper format likewise. (The following content is partly quoted from here )

· Template:

To: [address, exam it twice]
From: [real name]
Subject: [one line, brief and meaningful]

Dear/Hi [salution][first name],
[Head] Who I am, purpose of writing the email: challenge \ opportunity.
[Body] Explanation, description, or supporting information to explain \strengthen\justify your ask.
[Extension] Specific ask/next steps: meeting, action, etc. Timelines or date(if needed).

[Footer line]
[Regards]
[Sender's name]
[Sender's institution]
