    About blog

    · Inspiration

    I take myself as a creative person. I wish there could be a place comletely belonging to myself to hold thoughts. At the same time, I want to make up my deficiency in logical thinking. Later I found building a blog by myself may meet both of the two needs, so I started to establish the webpage by myself.

    I browsed many excellent programmers' blogs, two of which inspired me again, profoundly : Yihui Xie and Vamei. The former taught me to keep track of life experiences seriously; the latter gave me the bravey to genuinely write myself down.

    · Establishment

    I learn web-DIY by doing. Though I am a clumsy learner, I do enjoying making something new by myself, espeacilly with things are cool and mysterious. Basically HTML and CSS are enough to build a simple place like this, but I'll decorate it with some hand draw pictures and Javascript in the future. And I want to try Django as my backend framework. Ok,OK, don't be hurry...I am working on it.

    Notice: I grabbed Yihui's CSS stylesheet at first in order to get a quick understanding of web framework by imitation (I still use his font style and some layout ideas, thanks a lot). But I do not recommend this kind of attempt either because the finished web construction reflects individual's specific mindset and sometimes it can be rather sophisticated for a novice to read. It will be more efficient to realize your designing ideas step by step.
