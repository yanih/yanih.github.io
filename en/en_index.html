    About blog

    · Inspiration

    I take myself as a creative person. I wish there could be a place comletely belonging to myself to hold thoughts. At the same time, I want to make up my deficiency in logical thinking. Later I found building a blog may meet both of the two needs, so I started to establish the webpage by myself.

    I browsed many excellent programmers' blogs, two of which inspired me most, profoundly : Yihui Xie and Vamei. The former taught me to keep track of life experiences seriously; the latter gave me the bravery to genuinely write myself down.

    · Establishment

    Basic HTML and CSS are enough to build a simple place like this, but I'll decorate it with some hand draw pictures and Javascript in the future. Also, I want to try Django as my backend framework.

    · Intention

    In the Chinese part, I will focus on literature\random thoughts in life.
